### Shared Specifications

- **Power:** Single-phase AC 220V, 50/60Hz (voltage tolerance: -40% ~ +20%)
- **Max Pressure:** 1.0 MPa
- **Motor:** Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (PMSM)
- **Structure:** Canned / Shielded — no mechanical seal, zero leakage
- **Bearings:** High-hardness ceramic — maintenance-free
- **Certifications:** CE, RoHS, TÜV SÜD
- **OEM/ODM:** ✅ Private label, custom branding, and ODM design supported
- **MOQ:** 500 units (bulk orders)
- **Lead Time:** 3–30 days (varies by order type: samples, retail, bulk)
