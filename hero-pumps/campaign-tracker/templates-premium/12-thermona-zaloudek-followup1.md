Following up on circulating pumps for the THERM boiler line.

For wall-mounted condensing boilers, space and weight are critical. Our compact variable frequency circulating pumps are designed with this constraint in mind:

- Installation length: 130mm or 180mm (standard boiler fitting)
- Connection: G1¼ internal thread
- Weight: 2.1 kg (significantly lighter than cast iron alternatives)
- Maximum system pressure: 10 bar
- Fluid temperature range: 2°C to 110°C
- IP44 rated, insulation class F

The variable speed control means the pump adjusts to the boiler's modulation range automatically, improving part-load efficiency.
